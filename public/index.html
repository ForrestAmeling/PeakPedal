Why Us

Shop Confidently

We buy our bikes directly from Diamondback at a discounted price. Once you place an order with us, we place the order with Diamondback, and the product ships directly from Diamondback. It is the same bike you would buy off of their website, just discounted!

Save

Because we get our bikes discounted, we pass on our savings by reducing the price of the bike for you!

Returns

Returns follow Diamondbacks return policy. Product is in new condition, returned within 90 days after delivery, and is in original packaging. If bike is delivered damaged, pictures of damages will be required. Please email PeakPedalReturns@pm.me with your order number if you wish to make a return.
